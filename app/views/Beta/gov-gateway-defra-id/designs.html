Alpha Sign in / Create account with Government Gateway and Defra ID

Version 1

Sprint 0

User group:

Supervising engineers

Our Assumptions

  1. Due to the sensitivity of the information being entered, our users will understand the importance of securely logging in to Government Gateway and Defra accounts.
  2. They will need to do this to either sign in or create an account to submit an S12 Statement online.

The scenarios

Scenario 1

User wants to register for a service, no Gov Gateway or Defra ID accounts

Scenario 2

User has Gov Gateway account and no Defra ID account

Scenario 3

Returning user has both Gov Gateway and Defra accounts - two factor authentcation

What we tested

  • how the user would go about searching for our service online
  • can they sign into our online service
  • how to create a Defra ID account

What we learned

  • Users were confused by the option to register as an individual or a business
  • Some users were registered on the reservoir panel as an individual, but also worked for a company
  • All users passed through the Two-factor authentication (2FA) journey without any problems
