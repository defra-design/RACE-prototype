Private Beta S12 Annual statement template

User group: Supervising engineers

Sprint 2 – 3

Version 1

What we tested

  • To evaluate the content and structure of the S12 template
  • What do undertakers think of the proposed template?
  • What challenges, if any, do they foresee with receiving a report that is in the format of the new template?
  • What might improve the template for them?
  • What additional information might they need alongside the template?
  • To better understand the impact of a template on undertakers
  • How would the template fit with their current approach and processes?
  • How do they anticipate using the new template?
