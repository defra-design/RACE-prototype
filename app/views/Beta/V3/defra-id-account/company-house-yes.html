Where can I find the number?

You can find this on the Companies House register (opens in a new tab).

If you are applying on behalf of a company that is not registered in the UK, you need to change your account type to: Organisation that is not registered in the UK.

My company isn’t found

If you have only just registered your organisation it may take a few days for the information to be available to us through Companies House.

You can check with Companies House to see when your organisation is available.
