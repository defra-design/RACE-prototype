Monitoring actions for the operator

Monitoring action 1

Action

Sluice gate

Description

The sluice gate should be operated over at least 10 full turns twice annually and through its full travel at least once every 3 years, with a record of these operations added in the PFR.

Monitoring action 2

Action

Measure water levels

Description

Water levels should continue to be measured and recorded weekly. In addition, every effort should be made to measure and note in the record book the highest water level reached in the reservoir during any major flood, either by direct observation during the event or by subsequently observing the elevation of the wrack line relative to the overflow level.

Monitoring action 3

Action

Crest of the dam

Description

Settlement of the crest of the dam to be recorded every 5 years and as observed.

Monitoring action 4

Action

Leakage in pipe

Description

The area on the toe where the outlet pipe exists be watched for signs of any leakage.

Monitoring action 5

Action

Improving surveillance

Description

I recommend that the dam crest be visited no less than once every month by the Undertaker or their representative and a visual check carried out. Notify the supervising engineer if seepage, erosion or anything untoward is detected.
