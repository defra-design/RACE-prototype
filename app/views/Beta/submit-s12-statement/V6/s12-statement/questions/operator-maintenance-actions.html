Maintenance actions for the operator

These are the statutory maintenance measures in section10(3)(b).

Action 1

Action

Low-level outlet

What to do

The metal grill to the low-level outlet should be cleaned monthly and after significant floods.

Compliant

Yes

Action 2

Action

Grass levels

What to do

The grass on the perimeter embankment shall be mown periodically during the growing season.

Compliant

Yes

Action 3

Action

Debris build up

What to do

There is an accumulation in front of the spill weir and also upstream of the road culvert which needs to be cleared away promptly so as to prevent blockages.

Compliant

Yes

Action 4

Action

Spillway repair

What to do

The lower part of the formal spillway be repaired by replacing missing stones and repointing the masonry/brickwork where necessary.

Compliant

Yes

Action 5

Action

Trees to be removed

What to do

Remove all self seeded trees which are becoming established, where the trunk is less than 100mm in diameter.

Compliant

Yes
