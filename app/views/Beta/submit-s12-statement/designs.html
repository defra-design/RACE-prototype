What we tested

  • How might the prototype fit with their current ways of working?
  • What impact do they anticipate from using the new system? (positive and negative)
  • What challenges do they see with integrating the new process into the way they do things?
  • What might improve the prototype?
  • What opportunities do they see for a digital service (future thinking)?
  • Does the prototype allow for them to include all the information they would want to? (anything missing)
  • What information might they want to attach? (site visit reports)
  • What order does the information make most sense?
  • What are their thoughts on the quality of the service output for owners vs the report they would have originally created?
  • How well do they feel the output represents their work?
  • Are their concerns about the output that the owner will receive?
